Location: Arison Building  (the new building)

From the main entrance of IDC keep walking on the "main road" and when you see
the Caffeteria (on your right), take a left turn.
Walk until you see the entrance to Arison building.
The Reception hall is just at the entrance.
The lecture halls are at the corridor on the left side of the entrance.
Rest rooms are also available at the corridor.
