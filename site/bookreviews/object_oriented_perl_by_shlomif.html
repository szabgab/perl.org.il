Object Oriented Perl

Title: Object Oriented Perl
Author: Damian Conway
Publisher Manning
ISBN: 1884777791
Publishing date: August 1999
Pages: 512
Reviewer: Shlomi Fish
Review date: 2003 June
Rating:

I found the book moderately interesting. Maybe it's because I was already familiar with the material at hand. Damian Conway is a good writer, and his code examples are filled with interesting Perl techniques. Still, I found that I needed a lot of discipline to continue reading the book.

I'd probably recommend the book for people who are new to Object Oriented Programming, but not to people who are very familiar with it. Still, it's a high quality book.
